Event 1: Kumbh Mela

Date: April 1, 2023

Location: Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh

Join millions of pilgrims at the confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers for one of the largest religious gatherings, the Kumbh Mela.!

Event 2:Durga Puja

Date: October 5, 2023

Location: Kolkata, West Bengal

Experience the grandeur of Durga Puja, the major Hindu festival, with artistic idols, cultural performances, and vibrant processions in the streets of Kolkata.

Event 3: Pushkar Camel Fair

Date: November 8, 2023

Location: Pushkar, Rajasthan

Witness the spectacle of the Pushkar Camel Fair, a vibrant and unique celebration featuring cultural events, folk performances, and a massive camel trading market.

Event 4: Holi Festival

Date: March 17, 2024

Location: Across India

Immerse yourself in the colors of Holi, the festival of love and joy, as people come together to play with colored powders and celebrate the triumph of good over evil.

Event 5: Rann Utsav

Date: December 1, 2023, to February 28, 2024

Location: Kutch, Gujarat

Explore the cultural diversity of Gujarat at the Rann Utsav, a three-month-long festival featuring traditional dance, music, and the mesmerizing white desert landscape.
